# 《雾岭灯火》参考设定

## Core

《雾岭灯火》 is an original Chinese youth exploration and folk suspense series set in 青梧县 in summer 1998. The main cast investigates disappearances linked to 雾岭, 地下青梧, 引魂灯, old county records, a failed geophysical experiment, and folk performance traditions.

## Setting

青梧县 is a declining southwest mountain county. Important locations include the photo studio, county hospital, small store, cultural center archive, abandoned cinema, old air-raid tunnel, mountain shrine, reservoir relocation zone, and 雾岭.

地下青梧 is a damp inverted version of the county. Streets are familiar but wrong: reversed house numbers, low sky, no sun, old shops that no longer exist, and people who may be survivors or imitations.

## Rules

引魂灯 stabilizes the boundary. It is not simply a ghost lamp.

地下青梧 imitates people through memory and desire. The more a living person accepts an imitation as real, the stronger it becomes.

Photos, recordings, mirrors, shadow puppetry, old broadcasts, and opera lyrics can preserve overlap traces.

雾 signals a thin boundary; it is not automatically an entrance.

## Main Cast

林照野: 13, photo studio son. Sensitive, observant, carries an old camera. Wants to prove his father was not mad.

沈星河: 13, transfer student. Calm, technical, knows radios and old electronics. Her mother沈岚 hides the truth about her missing father.

马小满: 12, small-store kid. Talkative, afraid, funny, locally informed. His arc is ordinary courage.

顾南舟: 14, grandson of a retired martial opera performer. Impulsive and physical. His older brother顾北川 vanished three years earlier.

阿铃: looks 11, age unknown. Sings old lamp-opera, cannot read modern simplified Chinese well, and acts as a bridge between worlds.

Adults: 沈岚, 林成川, 赵砚秋.

## Season One Arc

周尧 disappears near 雾岭. The children find clues through a red rattle-drum, old cinema, air-raid tunnel, impossible map, and two versions of 周尧. Adult secrets reveal a failed experiment and an older ritual system. On the Ghost Festival night, lost people appear across town. The children extinguish the 引魂灯, rescue 周尧, lose 阿铃, and discover a photo of 沈星河's father holding a newly lit lamp.
